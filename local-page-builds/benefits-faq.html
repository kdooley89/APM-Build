BENEFITS - FAQ


Benefits


We understand you're not just choosing a place to work. You’re taking an important step in your career and your life.

That’s why we pride ourselves on our small company feel. Even if we’re a global operation, you are not just a number here. You’re joining an inclusive community of innovative, passionate, talented, committed individuals.

That talent, commitment and hard work is recognized by a competitive benefits package. We provide you the resources to ensure a healthy work/life balance – starting day one.

 

From healthcare plans and financial benefits to an understanding of the importance of a balanced life style. You get the right tools to manage all your life events.

Frequently Asked Questions



Need help applying?


If you are an individual with a disability or are limited in some capacity to apply online and require application assistance, please contact your local Compuware office or email our corporate recruiting department at recruiting@compuware.com.  Please note this is not to be used for checking on the status of a previously submitted application. Compuware is proud to be an EEO Employer - Minorities/Women/Disabled/Veterans.

What benefits does Compuware offer?


In the U.S., we offer a competitive benefits package for our salaried employees starting day one, from healthcare plans to financial benefits. Non-U.S. employees have the option of a comprehensive benefits package as well. Please visit our Benefits page for additional details.

What should I expect once I apply?


A recruiter will review your resume within two business days. If your background meets our current needs, typically the recruiter will contact you to schedule a phone screen. The initial phone screen may be followed by either a second phone screen or an on-site interview with the hiring manager(s). Once we’ve finished interviewing all the selected candidates and completed any reference and/or background checks, we try to make a final decision in a timely manner.

Are there any pre-employment screening tests I need to complete to work for Compuware?


Pre-employment screening tests vary per country and position. For more information, please contact your recruiter or recruiting@compuware.com.

What type of sponsorship does Compuware provide for foreign nationals seeking to work in the U.S.?


We regularly offer assistance to qualified candidates seeking a TN or H-1B visa (primarily transferring existing visas) and have an in-house legal team to support this initiative. As a U.S.-based company, we also support the Green Card process for those individuals who have longer-term residency goals.

I am having issues logging into my profile.


Forget your password or login name? Click here to reset your password and/or retrieve your login name. When you receive the password reset email, your login name will be included.
Password reset/login name retrieval instructions.

Do I need to provide my references up front or can I submit them later in the process?


We do not require a list of references with your initial resume submittal. However, reference checking is a part of our process (dependent on location and department).

How do I receive notifications when a job in my area of interest is available?


Connect with us! Join Compuware’s Talent Community and receive customized career information and alerts when opportunities are available. You can also sign up to receive event updates and career advice, plus general information about Compuware and the technology industry. You determine the information you'd like to receive and the frequency. You can also create job search agents by searching Compuware’s current openings, specific to your interests. Once the search results populate, name your search agent then click “Create Agent.” You can create multiple job search agents. Need further assistance? Click here.

I applied to a position but haven't heard back from a recruiter.


We monitor our resume database regularly and contact those whose background is in line with Compuware’s current needs. Unfortunately, we are unable to make personal contact with every candidate, however we will keep your resume on file for potential opportunities in the future. To stay connected with us and updated when opportunities you’re interested in become available, we encourage you to sign up for our Talent Community or create job search agents.

How should I prepare for my on-site interview?


Getting to the office: Office locations Dressing for your interview: Dress can vary depending on Compuware’s clients and offices. However, the traditional dress code for Compuware is professional. What to bring: Unless specified by your recruiter, other than your resume, no other documents are needed. It is recommended to bring enough copies of your resume to provide each person you will be interviewing with. As always, it is wise to do your homework before any interview!

Should I submit samples of my work with my resume?


For your initial resume submittal, please do not include work samples. However, if you have public copies of your work on line that you would like to share, you may include those links on your resume. If you are selected for an interview some groups may request samples of your work at that time. If you have any further questions, please email us at recruiting@compuware.com.
